Terms & Conditions

Last updated: January 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using WaitWise, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree, please do not use the system.

2. Use of the System

WaitWise is a queue management system for the University Registrar Office. You may only use it for legitimate registrar transactions. Misuse, including providing false information or abusing the queue system, may result in account suspension.

3. Account Responsibility

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. You must notify the registrar office immediately of any unauthorized use of your account.

4. Queue Conduct

Students must be present within the allowed time window when their queue number is called. Repeated no-shows may result in restrictions on future queue bookings.

5. Accuracy of Information

You agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration and when using the system.

6. Modifications

The university reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of WaitWise after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration: your name, student ID, course, email address, and phone number. We also collect queue transaction data including request type and wait times.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to manage your queue transactions, send notifications about your queue status, and improve the registrar service experience.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our university systems and retained for the duration of your enrollment and a reasonable period thereafter.

4. Notifications

By registering, you consent to receiving SMS and in-app notifications related to your queue status and registrar announcements.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties outside the university.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Please contact the Registrar Office to exercise these rights.
